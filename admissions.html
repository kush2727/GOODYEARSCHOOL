Admissions

Admission Process

1

Enquiry

Submit your enquiry form with basic information about your child and preferred class.

2

Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents including birth certificate and previous school records.

3

Interview

Attend the entrance test and interview session. Parents and students are both interviewed.

4

Enrollment

Upon selection, complete the enrollment process and pay the admission fee to secure your seat.

Eligibility Criteria

Class Age Group Eligibility Documents Required
Nursery 2.5 - 3.5 years No prior qualification required Birth Certificate, Vaccination Card
KG 3.5 - 4.5 years Nursery pass or equivalent Birth Certificate, Previous School Certificate
Class I 5 - 6 years KG pass or equivalent Birth Certificate, Previous School Certificate
Class II-V 7 - 11 years Previous class pass Birth Certificate, Previous School Certificate, Transfer Certificate
Class VI-VIII 11 - 14 years Previous class pass Birth Certificate, Previous School Certificate, Transfer Certificate
Class IX-XII 14 - 18 years Previous class pass Birth Certificate, Previous School Certificate, Transfer Certificate
