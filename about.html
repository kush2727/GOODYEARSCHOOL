About Us

Our Journey Since 1989

35 Years of Excellence in Education

Goodyear English School was established in 1989 with a vision to provide quality education and holistic development to students. Over the past 35 years, we have grown from a small institution to one of the most respected schools in the region.

Our commitment to academic excellence, character building, and overall personality development has made us a preferred choice for thousands of families. We have consistently maintained high standards in education while adapting to modern teaching methodologies and technological advancements.

  • Established in 1989 with a vision for excellence
  • Over 5000 successful alumni
  • Consistently ranked among top schools
  • Recognized for innovation in education

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops intellectually sound, morally upright, and socially responsible individuals who contribute positively to society and excel in their chosen fields.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development, nurturing global citizens who are prepared to face future challenges with confidence and integrity.
